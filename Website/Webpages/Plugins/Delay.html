Delay

A simple yet powerful delay with Tone and Warmth controls to emulate the sound of an analogue tape delay.

Delay is an essential effect in any use case, whether it be for processing vocals or adding interest to a piano recording. Chroma Delay Offers a wide range possibilities for sound design and music production. Using the Sync control, you can choose whether to quantise each delay to the tempo of the project. Alongside this, Tone and Warmth controls allow you to add subtle saturation and filtering to you sound emulating the sound of analogue hardware.
